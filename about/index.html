About Us

Crafting beautiful cakes with passion and artistry since day one

Our journey began with a simple frustration — being lifelong vegetarians, we struggled to find delicious cakes that were truly eggless. Every celebration felt incomplete because the options just weren’t there. So we decided to change that. At our bakery, every cake is crafted to make sure no one has to compromise on taste, tradition, or values.

We specialize exclusively in 100% eggless cakes, baked fresh with high‑quality ingredients and a whole lot of love. What started as a personal need has grown into a mission: to make celebrations inclusive, joyful, and absolutely delicious for everyone.
